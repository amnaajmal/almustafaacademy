Welcome to the Admission

How to Apply

  • Open Online Admission Form by using the given link in your e-mail.
  • Complete your application details step by step.
  • Submit application processing fee through system generated fee voucher.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED WITH THE APPLICATION FORM:

  • CNIC / Form-B
  • Father’s & Mother’s CNIC

PHOTOGRAPH SPECIFICATION

  • The picture should be clearly visible.
  • Background should be on plain white or off white.
  • The contrast and lighting in the photo should be normal
  • The picture should have a minimum resolution of 600 pixels x 450 pixels
  • The format of the file should be JPEG, JPG, PNG (file size less than 1 MB)

Note

Kindly upload your recent passport size photograph. Selfie and informal/ causal pictures are not
